Delete Your TISSCA Account

If you would like to delete your TISSCA account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email: support@tissca.com
  2. Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address and full name

What will be deleted:

  • Account profile information
  • Leads and job data
  • Messages and workspace data

Data retention:

Financial records may be retained where legally required (up to 6 years in the UK). All other personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of confirmation.
